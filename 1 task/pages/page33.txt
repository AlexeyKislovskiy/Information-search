<p>The <b>Bahnar language</b> or Ba-Na language (<style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1177148991">.mw-parser-output .IPA-label-small{font-size:85%}.mw-parser-output .references .IPA-label-small,.mw-parser-output .infobox .IPA-label-small,.mw-parser-output .navbox .IPA-label-small{font-size:100%}</style><span class="IPA-label IPA-label-small">Vietnamese pronunciation:</span> <span class="IPA nowrap" lang="vi-Latn-fonipa"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Vietnamese" title="Help:IPA/Vietnamese">&#91;βəna:&#93;</a></span>)  is a Central <a href="/wiki/Bahnaric_languages" title="Bahnaric languages">Bahnaric language</a>. It has nine <a href="/wiki/Vowel_quality" class="mw-redirect" title="Vowel quality">vowel qualities</a> and phonemic <a href="/wiki/Vowel_length" title="Vowel length">vowel length</a>.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Subdivisions">Subdivisions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bahnar_language&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Subdivisions"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Đào (1998)<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> lists the following subgroups of Bahnar and their respective locations.
</p>
<ul><li><b>Bahnar <a href="/wiki/Kon_Tum_Province" class="mw-redirect" title="Kon Tum Province">Kon Tum</a></b>: <a href="/wiki/Kon_Tum" title="Kon Tum">Kon Tum</a> area</li>
<li><b>Bahnar Jơlong</b>: northeastern <a href="/wiki/Kon_Tum_Province" class="mw-redirect" title="Kon Tum Province">Kon Tum Province</a></li>
<li><b>Bahnar Golar</b>: northern <a href="/wiki/Pleiku" title="Pleiku">Pleiku</a> (Gia Lai Province)</li>
<li><b>Bahnar Tosung</b>: <a href="/wiki/Mang_Yang_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Mang Yang District">Mang Yang District</a> (Gia Lai Province)</li>
<li><b>Bahnar Konkođe</b>: <a href="/wiki/An_Kh%C3%AA_District" class="mw-redirect" title="An Khê District">An Khê District</a> (Gia Lai Province)</li>
<li><b>Bahnar Alatang</b>: <a href="/wiki/An_Kh%C3%AA_District" class="mw-redirect" title="An Khê District">An Khê District</a> (Gia Lai Province)</li>
<li><b>Bahnar Alakông</b>: <a href="/wiki/K%27Bang_District" class="mw-redirect" title="K&#39;Bang District">K'Bang District</a> (Gia Lai Province)</li>
<li><b>Bahnar Tolo</b>: <a href="/wiki/K%C3%B4ng_Chro_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Kông Chro District">Kông Chro District</a> and southern <a href="/wiki/An_Kh%C3%AA_District" class="mw-redirect" title="An Khê District">An Khê District</a> (Gia Lai Province)</li>
<li><b>Bahnar Bơnâm</b>: eastern <a href="/wiki/An_Kh%C3%AA_District" class="mw-redirect" title="An Khê District">An Khê District</a> (Gia Lai Province)</li>
<li><b>Bahnar Roh</b>: scattered areas in <a href="/wiki/An_Kh%C3%AA_District" class="mw-redirect" title="An Khê District">An Khê District</a> and <a href="/wiki/K%27Bang_District" class="mw-redirect" title="K&#39;Bang District">K'Bang District</a> (Gia Lai Province)</li>
<li><b>Bahnar Krem</b>: <a href="/wiki/V%C4%A9nh_Th%E1%BA%A1nh_District,_B%C3%ACnh_%C4%90%E1%BB%8Bnh" class="mw-redirect" title="Vĩnh Thạnh District, Bình Định">Vĩnh Thạnh District</a> and <a href="/wiki/An_L%C3%A3o_District,_B%C3%ACnh_%C4%90%E1%BB%8Bnh" class="mw-redirect" title="An Lão District, Bình Định">An Lão District</a> (Bình Định Province)</li>
<li><b>Bahnar Chăm</b>: <a href="/wiki/V%C3%A2n_Canh_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Vân Canh District">Vân Canh District</a> (Bình Định Province)</li>
<li><b>Bahnar But</b>: forest areas; little communication with outsiders</li></ul>
<p>Bùi (2011:5-6)<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> lists the following subgroups of Bahnar and their respective locations.
</p>
<ul><li><b>Tơ Lô</b>: in Sơ Ró, Đắc Song, Chơ Loong, Yang Nam, and Đắc Tơ communes, which are in <a href="/wiki/K%C3%B4ng_Chro_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Kông Chro District">Kông Chro District</a> and part of <a href="/wiki/An_Kh%C3%AA_District" class="mw-redirect" title="An Khê District">An Khê District</a> (Gia Lai Province)</li>
<li><b>Krem</b>: in <a href="/wiki/V%C4%A9nh_Th%E1%BA%A1nh_District,_B%C3%ACnh_%C4%90%E1%BB%8Bnh" class="mw-redirect" title="Vĩnh Thạnh District, Bình Định">Vĩnh Thạnh District</a>, as well as some in <a href="/wiki/An_L%C3%A3o_District,_B%C3%ACnh_%C4%90%E1%BB%8Bnh" class="mw-redirect" title="An Lão District, Bình Định">An Lão District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ho%C3%A0i_%C3%82n_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Hoài Ân District">Hoài Ân District</a>, and <a href="/wiki/T%C3%A2y_S%C6%A1n_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Tây Sơn District">Tây Sơn District</a> in Bình Định Province; some in Sơn Lang Commune, <a href="/wiki/K%27Bang_District" class="mw-redirect" title="K&#39;Bang District">K'Bang District</a>, Gia Lai Province</li>
<li><b>Vân Canh</b>: mostly in Canh Liên, Canh Thuận, and Canh Hòa communes of <a href="/wiki/V%C3%A2n_Canh_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Vân Canh District">Vân Canh District</a>, Bình Định Province</li>
<li><b>Thồ Lồ</b>: mostly in the upper part of <a href="/wiki/%C4%90%E1%BB%93ng_Xu%C3%A2n_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Đồng Xuân District">Đồng Xuân District</a>, Phú Yên Province. Scattered in 9 communes throughout <a href="/wiki/%C4%90%E1%BB%93ng_Xu%C3%A2n_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Đồng Xuân District">Đồng Xuân District</a>, <a href="/wiki/S%C6%A1n_H%C3%B2a_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Sơn Hòa District">Sơn Hòa District</a>, and <a href="/wiki/S%C3%B4ng_Hinh_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Sông Hinh District">Sông Hinh District</a>. Descended from Tơ Lô people who were <a href="/wiki/T%C3%A2y_S%C6%A1n_dynasty" title="Tây Sơn dynasty">Tây Sơn</a> soldiers fleeing from the <a href="/wiki/Nguy%E1%BB%85n_dynasty" title="Nguyễn dynasty">Nguyễn dynasty</a> 200 years ago. They fled southeast to Thồ Lồ, Phú Yên Province. The</li>
<li><b>Gơ Lar</b>: mostly in <a href="/wiki/Mang_Yang_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Mang Yang District">Mang Yang District</a>, <a href="/wiki/%C4%90%E1%BA%AFk_%C4%90oa_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Đắk Đoa District">Đắk Đoa District</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ch%C6%B0_S%C3%AA_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Chư Sê District">Chư Sê District</a> of Gia Lai Province, and some living around <a href="/wiki/Kon_Tum" title="Kon Tum">Kon Tum</a> town. There is also a subgroup called <b>Bahnar Châng H'drung</b> who live at the foot of Hàm Rồng Mountain.</li>
<li><b>Kon Tum</b>: in 4 precincts and 9 communes of <a href="/wiki/Kon_Tum" title="Kon Tum">Kon Tum</a> town</li>
<li><b>Jơ Lơng</b>: in <a href="/wiki/Kon_Tum" title="Kon Tum">Kon Tum</a> town, and Đắc Tơ Re, Đắc Ruồng, and Đắc Pne communes of <a href="/wiki/Kon_R%E1%BA%ABy_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Kon Rẫy District">Kon Rẫy District</a>, Kon Tum Province</li>
<li><b>Rơ Ngao</b> (<a href="/wiki/Rengao_language" title="Rengao language">Rengao</a>): in 29 villages. They live with the Kon Tum people in 5 communes of <a href="/wiki/Kon_Tum" title="Kon Tum">Kon Tum</a>; with the Bahnar and Sedang peoples in 2 communes of <a href="/wiki/%C4%90%E1%BA%AFk_H%C3%A0_District" class="mw-redirect" title="Đắk Hà District">Đắk Hà District</a>; and with the <a href="/wiki/Sedang_people" class="mw-redirect" title="Sedang people">Sedang people</a> in Pô Cô Commune, <a href="/wiki/%C4%90%E1%BA%AFk_T%C3%B4" class="mw-redirect" title="Đắk Tô">Đắk Tô</a> District, Kon Tum Province</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Also_used_in_Vietnam">Also used in Vietnam</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bahnar_language&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Also used in Vietnam"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<li>Jolong (Gio-Lang, Y-Lang)</li>
